About the band

Title Fight is an American punk rock band, formed in 2003 in Kingston, Pennsylvania.
Througout the realease of their 3 sudio albums, their music has gradually shifted from being hardcore punk-oriented, towards having an emphasis on mellow atmospheres.
Aside from a few select appearances, the band has been mostly inactive since 2016.

Current members

Title Fight was initially formed as a three-piece band consisting of Jamie Rhoden, and the twin brothers Ned and Ben Russin. After the later incorporation of Shane Moran, they have produced more than 20 miscellaneous releases and toured through the USA, Japan, Australia, and Europe.

Discography and musical style

Their music has been described as post-hardcore, hardcore punk, melodic hardcore, punk rock, emo, shoegaze and post-rock. Since their formation as a harcore band, they soon started to draw influences from a variety of other genres such as alternative rock, shoegaze, and post-rock.
Title Fight has released 3 studio albums, a compilation album, and multiple singles, EPs, splits and music videos.
